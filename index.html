Red Hat JBoss Middleware Hands-On Labs, join us at one of our Benelux locations

Have you ever wondered how to get started with the Red Hat JBoss Middleware products? Are you not sure where to begin? You like to receive information, but prefer to work with the products to get your hands ''dirty''? Join us for one of our free JBoss Hands-on Labs, (formerly known as our Red Hat in-house Hackathons). We are providing you with different topics on several locations. Please see the overview for the first half year below:

January 29th JBoss Data Grid - Amsterdam 
Red Hat JBoss Data Grid is an intelligent distributed caching solution. Red Hat JBoss Data Grid supercharges today's modern applications, allowing them to meet trough requirements of high performance, availability, reliability and elastic scale. Red Hat JBoss Data Grid handles unprecedented transaction volumes, meet high uptime requirements, deploy into hybrid cloud environments, provide access to accurate, real-time information and streamline interaction with complex and rigid data tiers.

February 11th JBoss A-MQ - Amsterdam 
Red Hat JBoss A-MQ is a standards-based messaging platform that makes it easy and safe for enterprise applications to exchange information. Using various messaging patterns to support real-time messaging, Red Hat JBoss A-MQ integrates application, endpoints, and devices quickly and efficiently. As a result, your enterprise is more responsive and agile.

March 5th JBoss Data Virtualization - Amsterdam
Do you have data from multiple sources that you need to unify into a single trusted, accurate, accountable source? Need to integrate new and changed data sources in your application infrastructure? Get to know Red Hat JBoss Data Virtualization, the best way to work with data across several systems.

Learn how to get started with JBoss Data Grid, JBoss A-MQ and JBoss Data Virtualization and register for our free hands-on lab by sending and email to marketingbenelux@redhat.com

We are looking forward seeing you there!
